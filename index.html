Tagore Dance

Tagore's strategy was to synthesize the techniques Southeast Asian styles, such as the Javanese and Balinese and occasionally Kandy dance of Sri Lanka and Tagore's own dance styles. At the same time, he also looked towards folk dances to lend vigour to per- formances. Which is only been done with Tagore's own Songs and Dramas. Which now nation wise known as RABINDRA NRITYA .
